__CAT + SIENNA'S BLACKJACK!__

- This is 1-player Blackjack, so it's just you and the dealer.

*Objective*
Get a hand total as close to 21 as possible without going over (that's called a "bust"), and have a higher total than the dealer.

*Card Values*
Number cards (2–10) are worth their face value.
Jack, Queen, and King are worth 10.
Ace is worth 1 or 11, whichever benefits the hand more.

*The Deal*
Player and dealer receive two cards face up.

*Player’s Turn*
You can either: 
    Hit: Take another card.
    Stand: Keep the current hand and end the turn.

*Dealer's (Computer's) Turn*
The computer will hit if the total is 16 or less.
The computer will stand if the total is 17 or more.

*Winning*
- If anybody busts, they automatically lose.
- If a player’s total is higher than the computer’s without going over 21, the player wins.
- If the computer’s total is higher without going over 21, the player loses.
- If both totals are the same, it is a push.
